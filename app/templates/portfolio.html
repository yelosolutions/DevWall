Idustrial Attachment Management System

An Industrial Attachment Management System(IAMS) is a complete web application. IAMS implements data storage, a back-end and a front-end interfacing. It enables undergraduate students to select a company,apply for attachment and register their details and of their supervisors. It links industries with learning institutions, easing the placement of students in workplaces for the acquisition of practical skills and appropriate work-ethics. A student will therefore be able to easily apply for an attachment position and get placed to the respective institutions or industries.
